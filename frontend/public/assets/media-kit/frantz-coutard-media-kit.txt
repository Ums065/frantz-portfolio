FRANTZ COUTARD - OFFICIAL MEDIA KIT

Short Bio
Frantz Coutard is an award-winning entrepreneur, technology innovator, and community advocate based in New York. He is the CEO and Founder of TrendCatch Network and the Founder and President of TrendCatch Gives Back Inc. His work focuses on local commerce, community empowerment, and building technology that helps people and neighborhoods thrive.

Core Story
Born in Haiti and arriving in the United States in 1997 without speaking English, Frantz built his career through faith, discipline, and persistence. In November 2018, he launched TrendCatch Digital Advertising to help local businesses gain affordable visibility. In January 2026, that vision expanded into TrendCatch Network, a broader ecosystem designed to connect businesses, consumers, schools, nonprofits, and retailers.
